About Vidya Niketan Public School

A Legacy of Educational Excellence Since 1997

Our Journey

Founded in 1997, Vidya Niketan Public School began as a humble institution with a grand vision - to provide quality English-medium education to the children of Bagthala and surrounding areas in Rewari, Haryana.

Over the past 27+ years, we have grown from a small school with just a handful of students to a prestigious educational institution serving over 1000 students from Nursery to Class X.

Affiliated with the Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE) under Registration Number 24943, we have consistently maintained high standards of academic excellence and holistic development.
