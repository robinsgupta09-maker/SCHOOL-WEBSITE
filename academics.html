🏆 Navodaya Vidyalaya Coaching Program

A Special Initiative for Talented Students

What is Navodaya Vidyalaya?

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) are a system of central schools in India, established to provide quality education to talented students from rural areas. Admission is through a highly competitive entrance exam (JNVST) conducted by NVS.

How We Prepare Students

  • ✓ Special coaching classes for JNVST preparation
  • ✓ Mental Ability Training
  • ✓ Arithmetic & Mathematical Reasoning Practice
  • ✓ Language Test Preparation (Hindi & English)
  • ✓ Regular Mock Tests & Practice Papers
  • ✓ Time Management & Exam Strategy Sessions

Program Highlights

🎯 Success Rate

Consistently high selection rate with multiple students qualifying JNVST every year.

📚 Duration

Year-round coaching program for Class 5 and Class 6 students.

👨‍🏫 Expert Faculty

Dedicated experienced teachers specializing in competitive exam preparation.
