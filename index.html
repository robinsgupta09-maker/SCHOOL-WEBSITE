Welcome to Vidya Niketan Public School

Founded in 1997, Vidya Niketan Public School has been a beacon of quality education in Bagthala, Rewari. Affiliated with the Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE), we provide comprehensive education from Nursery to Class X, nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and holistic approach to education ensure that every student receives the attention and guidance they need to excel academically and personally.
